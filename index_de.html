Der Ethereum Computer

ein sicheres Tor zum Web 3.0

Eine kleiner, vorinstallierter und vorkonfigurierter Home-Server, auf dem sowohl eine optimierte Ethereum-Node, wie auch viele dezentralisierte Apps laufen, z.B.: ein web3 ID Vault, Slock Schnittstellen zu vielen Geräten, wie auch einige experimentelle Software einschließlich eines IPFS Server, met dem sogaer ein paar Ether verdienen könnte.

Unsere erste Version des Ethereum Computers wird eine kryptographisch sicheren Umgebung sein, welche Heimautomatisierung neu definiert und trotzdem populäre Protokolle wie Z-Wave und ZigBee unterstützt und es damit ermöglicht das Haus oder Büro zu vermieten.

Der Ethereum Computer bringt Smart Contracts in ihr Haus, die sicher in alle Geräte integrieren können und bequem mit jedem SmartPhone oder PC steuern können. convenience of your desktop or home theater.

Governed by a DAO

A new technology for a new economy

The Slock.it DAO will issue tokens to those who helped bring the project to life, granting rewards when smart objects and property are put to use.

Transparent

Slock.it is a DAO, or Decentralized Autonomous Organization, a digital company with its by-laws set immutably within the Blockchain. It's the most transparent form of organization there can be.

Integrated

Slock.it's very own token presale is itself governed by a smart contract, so you'll be able to track its progress on the Blockchain. Of course, it's all open source.

Rewarding

Backers of the project will be able to call the Slock.it DAO smart contract directly and receive their share of the rewards proportional to their contribution - at any time, and for ever.

Simple

The Slock.it smart contract is embedded on the Ethereum blockchain, and operates entirely autonomously and 'trustlessly'. To make it easier to read, we will annotate it with Genius (1, 2)

Roadmap

June 2015

The Story Begins

From the day of the project's inception, our team will spend the following months in stealth mode relentlessly developing, building and testing prototypes.

November 12th 2015

Slock.it Revealed

The project is introduced at Devcon One in front of 400 blockchain developers and enthusiasts. Our first product, the SHS (now renamed the 'Ethereum Computer'), is announced, and demoed opening doors and powering up electrical appliances.

Early 2016

Slock token presale

An innovative presale to bootstrap the world's first DAO. Operating entirely autonomously and transparently, the DAO will elect a service provider to execute on a series of core objectives in the physical world, to be reviewed at regular intervals.

2017

Launch!

The first Slocks enter the market. To reward the participants who helped bring it to life, the DAO issued Slock Tokens granting rewards whenever Slocks are put to use.

A Bright Future

A World of a 100 Billion Devices

We started Slock.it with realistic targets and the simplest of all use cases: the humble lock, or ‘slock’, consuming very little of the processing power available to today's SoCs.

Slock.it therefore lends itself very well to deeper IoT integration, both in the commercial and industrial sectors, where we are piloting networks of autonomous devices operating as part of an Economy of Things, engaging in contracts with each other, paying each other for their services, yet never having to be centrally managed.
